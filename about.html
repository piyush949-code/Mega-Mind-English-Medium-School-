About MMEMS

Mega Mind English Medium School set amidst sylvan surroundings with vast open space and beautiful, bright, spacious, well ventilated, aesthetically designed school building is a haven for children to retreat into and start off on an adventure into learning. The serenity and calmness of the atmosphere soothes the child and is conducive to learning. The colourful classrooms, activity rooms, art studio, indoor and outdoor play areas are designed to create a feeling of joy and happiness in the child as he/she steps into the school portal and the transition from home to school becomes a smooth one. At MMES, children grow and develop into healthy, happy, creative and responsible global citizens. The education is 'child centric' and every child acquires knowledge, values and life skills as the environment is endlessly inspiring, enlightening and stimulating. It makes the children want to come to school every day.
