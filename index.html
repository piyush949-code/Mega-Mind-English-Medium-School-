Welcome to MMES

Mega Mind English Medium School is a English School , situated at kharki-road, Pansemal, Dist- Barwani, Madhypradesh, India.


It set beautiful, bright, spacious, well ventilated, aesthetically designed school building is a nice place for children to retreat into and start off on an adventure into learning.
